We Love Each Other

Our Story

Our paths crossed by fate, grew with love, and now unite in a lifelong promise.

  • First We Meet

    May 25, 2020

    Our story began at Intelmetic Office, Kolkata. During the pandemic, when work from home was the norm, we first saw each other on a webcam. What started as a simple introduction slowly turned into something special. When the office reopened and we finally met in person, little did we know that this meeting would be the beautiful beginning of our forever.

  • First Date

    September 5, 2021

    As our journeys at Intelmetic were coming to an end, with both of us serving our notice periods, we decided to step outside the office walls and create a memory of our own. That special day at Maidan, in front of the majestic Victoria Memorial, became our very first date. The evening was filled with warmth, laughter, and a feeling we both knew was just the beginning of something beautiful.

  • Our First Durga Puja Visit

    October 9, 2021

    On the 9th of October, we shared a delightful lunch with friends at Kareem's on Park Street, Kolkata. The evening unfolded with the magic of Durga Puja as we went pandal hopping, capturing beautiful memories along the way. With hearts full of joy and laughter, I returned to Bankura after a perfect day, cherishing the moments that marked the beginning of our journey together.

  • In a Relationship

    August 5, 2021 - Forever

    From that moment on, our journey blossomed into love. Every laugh, every memory, and every little adventure brought us closer, turning friendship into a bond we cherish forever.
